Citing NASLib¶

If you use this code in your own work, please Ruchte et al. 1 and White et al. 2.

1

Michael Ruchte, Arber Zela, Julien Siems, Josif Grabocka, and Frank Hutter. Naslib: a modular and flexible neural architecture search library. https://www.github.com/automl/NASLib, 2020.

2

Colin White, Arber Zela, Binxin Ru, Yang Liu, and Frank Hutter. How powerful are performance predictors in neural architecture search? arXiv preprint arXiv:2104.01177, 2021.
